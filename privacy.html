Privacy Policy

Effective 1 July 2025  ·  Eduxellence Solutions Bright Horizon, Abuja, Nigeria

Eduxellence Solutions Bright Horizon is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our website, platforms, and services. By using our services, you consent to the practices described here.

1Who We Are

Eduxellence Solutions Bright Horizon is a professional services company registered in Nigeria, providing research assistance, data analysis, digital marketing, and education technology solutions including the School Results Portal at results.eduxellence.org.

For data protection purposes, we act as the data controller for information collected through our website and consulting engagements, and as a data processor for student data uploaded by schools to the Results Portal.

3How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver and improve our consulting services and platforms.
  • Communicate with you about your project, account, or service package.
  • Process payments and maintain transaction records.
  • Provide customer support and respond to enquiries.
  • Send service-related notifications (delivery confirmations, platform updates).
  • Comply with legal obligations and detect/prevent fraud.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4Student Data (Results Portal)

Schools using the Results Portal upload student data as part of their academic management. In this context:

  • The school is the data controller — responsible for obtaining consent and ensuring lawful use of student data.
  • We are the data processor — we process student data only on the school's instructions.
  • Student data is stored securely via Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security and encryption at rest.
  • We do not share student data with third parties except as required to operate the platform.
  • Schools may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

6Data Retention

  • Consulting client data: 2 years after project completion, then securely deleted.
  • Results Portal data: Duration of active subscription plus 6 months.
  • Payment records: 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations.
  • Enquiry/contact form data: 12 months.

7Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to maintain login sessions on the Results Portal, remember basic preferences, and collect anonymous usage statistics. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

8Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, request data portability, or withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, email j.sylvester@eduxellence.org. We will respond within 30 days.

9Data Security

Our security measures include HTTPS encryption on all web platforms, password hashing (bcrypt), row-level security on the Results Portal database, access controls limiting who can view client and student data, and regular security reviews. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

10International Data Transfers

Our services are hosted on infrastructure that may be located outside Nigeria (including the United States and European Union). By using our services, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in these jurisdictions.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. For material changes, we will notify active platform users via email. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
